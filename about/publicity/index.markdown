### Developer Interviews and Talks






Talk at MeeGo Conference 2010

    Aaron Bockover gave a talk at MeeGo Conference 2010, on Monday November 15, 2010 in Dublin.   [Read the abstract, watch the talk](http://conference2010.meego.com/session/dive-banshee-meego-netbook-media-player-0) (missing audio first couple minutes)


Talk at GUADEC 2010

    Gabriel Burt gave a talk at GUADEC, the annual GNOME conference, on Friday July 30, 2010 in The Hague.  [View slides](http://download.banshee.fm/documents/2010-GUADEC-Banshee_Awesome_Factory.pdf) / [Watch the talk](http://ubuntudevelopers.blip.tv/file/4363767/)


Banshee Accessibility at CSUN 2010

    Eitan Isaacson gave a talk at [CSUN 2010](http://www.csunconference.org/) in San Diego about his experience spearheading Banshee's accessibility effort.  [View slides](http://people.gnome.org/~eitani/csun2010)


Interview on Hanselminutes

    Aaron Bockover was [interviewed on June 30, 2009 by Scott Hanselman](http://www.hanselminutes.com/default.aspx?showID=186) on his podcast [Hanselminutes](http://www.hanselminutes.com/) about Banshee and cross-platform .NET app development.


Talk at Gran Canaria Desktop Summit 2009

    Banshee developer Aaron Bockover gave [a talk](http://www.grancanariadesktopsummit.org/node/206) at the [Gran Canaria Desktop Summit of 2009](http://2009.guadec.org/).  He blogged [about his talk](http://abock.org/2009/07/14/exciting-updates-on-the-road-to-banshee-2-0) and posted his slides.


Chicago LUG - October 2008

    Gabriel Burt gave a [talk](http://gburt.blogspot.com/2008/10/talkative-banshee.html).


Utah Open Source Conference - August 2008

    Aaron Bockover gave a [talk](http://2008.utosc.com/presentation/117/).


Talks at GUADEC 2008

    Banshee developers Aaron Bockover and Gabriel Burt gave a talk in Istanbul at [GUADEC 2008](http://2008.guadec.org/), the annual [GNOME](http://gnome.org/) conference.  They gave demos of Banshee's features, talked about how easy Banshee is to extend, and discussed plans for the next couple releases.  Their [slides are available](http://download.banshee-project.org/documents/GUADEC-2008-Banshee_Talk.pdf).

    Banshee maintainers Aaron Bockover and Gabriel Burt gave a talk at LugRadio Live on April 12, 2008 in San Francisco. Banshee 1.0 Alpha 3 is demoed.
