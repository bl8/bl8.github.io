[Banshee 1.4.2](http://banshee-project.org/download/archives/1.4.2/) is a stable release with many bug fixes and minor improvements.  Fixed and improved are RSS feed parsing, library rescanning, occasional startup freezes, launching on device insertion, several crashers, and improved playback on OS X (still a technology preview/alpha release).

[Get it now!](http://banshee-project.org/download)
