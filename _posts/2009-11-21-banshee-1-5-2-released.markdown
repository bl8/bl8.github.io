[Banshee 1.5.2](http://banshee-project.org/download/archives/1.5.2/), aka 1.6 beta 3, was released with several new features and many fixes, including an iTunes importer, improved accessibility, reading/writing of playcounts and ratings, and more supported devices.

[Get it now!](http://banshee-project.org/download)
