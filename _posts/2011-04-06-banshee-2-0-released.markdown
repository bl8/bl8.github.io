[Banshee 2.0](http://banshee-project.org/download/archives/2.0.0/) has been released!  Banshee 2.0 is the culmination of six months' work by 36 developers and dozens of translators, bug reporters, and testers. It is a stable release, the successor to Banshee 1.8.  It has some exciting new features and lots of polish!  Read the [release notes](http://banshee-project.org/download/archives/2.0.0/) for more info.

[Get it now!](http://banshee-project.org/download)
