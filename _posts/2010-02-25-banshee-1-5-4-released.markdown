[Banshee 1.5.4](http://banshee-project.org/download/archives/1.5.4/), aka 1.6 beta 5, was released with several new features and many fixes, including equalizer presets, Nokia N900 support, opt-in/anonymous usage-data collection, and support for the new Banshee Community Extensions sub-project.

[Get it now!](http://banshee-project.org/download)
