This [latest release](http://banshee-project.org/download/archives/1.4.1/) brings out-of-the-box support for HTC G1 (Android) phones (complete with Amazon MP3 purchase importing, cover art syncing), the first technology preview release for Mac OS X, a new sexy track editor, media player playlist support and automated sync, collection rescanning, shiny UI updates, and performance and stability fixes! [Get it now!](http://banshee-project.org/download)
