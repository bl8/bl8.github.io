[Banshee 2.6](http://banshee-project.org/download/archives/2-6-0/) has been released!  

Banshee 2.6 is the culmination of six months' work by 15 developers, 30 translators and dozens of bug reporters and testers. It is a stable release, the successor to Banshee 2.4.  

It has some exciting new features, particularly on OS X and lots of polish!  Read the [release notes](http://banshee-project.org/download/archives/2-6-0/) for more info.  

[Get it now!](http://banshee-project.org/download)
