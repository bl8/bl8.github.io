Banshee 1.8 is just [one week away](http://banshee.fm/about/calendar/)!  Compared to [1.6](http://banshee.fm/download/archives/1.6.0/), it has some awesome advances.  You can help us polish and perfect it by testing the [1.7.6 release](http://banshee.fm/download/archives/1.7.6/) or [git master](http://banshee-project.org/download/development/) and filing bugs, or by [translating it](http://banshee.fm/contribute/translate/) into your language.  Join us on our mailing list/forum or on IRC chat to [get in touch](http://banshee.fm/about/contact/)!
